The test isn't hard.

Signal-based preparation for students who need to move their score efficiently. Personalized tactics, logic formulas, and decision-making skills that transfer far beyond test day.

The Approach

Precision over
volume.

The Lettuce Learn method trains students to clarify the task before committing any attention, identify the signal — the 20% of content that answers 80% of the question — and filter the noise deliberately. The result is a student who moves through the test with clarity and confidence, not speed for its own sake.

Every session is personalized. Every tactic is built around the individual student's learning profile, not a generic curriculum.

The Process

Every engagement
starts with a Game Plan.

Before a single session begins, every student goes through a structured diagnostic evaluation. This isn't to test and place the student. It's so that we first learn how the student sees the test.

01
Diagnostic Test
A full-length, timed diagnostic under real test conditions. 60 minutes. The starting point for everything.
60 minutes · Self-proctored
02
Evaluation Session
A 60-minute session with the Chief Strategist to assess strengths, identify knowledge gaps, surface logical miscues, and determine whether SAT or ACT is the stronger path.
60 minutes · With Chief Strategist
03
The Game Plan
Your student receives a Projection Report — a projected score, optimal test date, and a personalized study plan. The Game Plan is the blueprint for every session that follows.
Delivered within 48 hours
